Nicolás Caradzoglu Studio

Caradzoglu Estudio is a modern legal firm from Buenos Aires, specialized in taxation law. Their values are formed primary by Efficiency, Trust, Loyalty and Success.

They needed a brand design that would represent their values and commitment, and basic landing page to present themselves.

Beeing a modern law firm, the logo needed to be classic yet modern and minimal, to appeal to all ages. It ought to be distinguishable and highly communicative inspite of its simpleness.

The Logo

I started working on the logo, putting black on white text to visualise the shape of the full name, trying it in different typographies. The design had to be modern and not too complex, in this case classic and minimalist would work best.

On the final version, the definitive Logo are two: the first one is composed by the full name of the partener in elegant and contrastive serif typography, keeping a broad letter spacing giving it a softer vibe opposing the firmness of the typo itself.

The second logo is a monogram that consists in the first and last letters C and Z of the partner surname. Since this may be a long name for somke uses, I needed to create a more compact and simpler version. It follows the same typography, by keeping the C a bit bigger than the Z, and the latter one joining the C like if it's a hook.

Both versions can be visualized in dark letters over white background, for example, in the use of personalized stationery. Or it can be white letters on a dark grey background, best use for websites, platform’s profiles or posts, etc.

Typography & Colors Palette

The main typography used in the logo and accents is an elegant and very classic with semi-rounded edges. It states As a second font, it was pick a sans-serif modern sleek one, Open Sans, to state the body texts on the website and the signature of the logo.

For the colors palette I opted for subdued colors, keeping a pure white, and adding a dark blueish grey for a semi-dark shade, a softer contrast in comparison to pure black. I considered this grey hue a great color for the website’s background.
