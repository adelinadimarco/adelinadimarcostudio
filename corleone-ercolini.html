The Company

Erercolini & Corleone & Associates is Legal firm funded in 1992 in Buenos Aires, Argentina, specialized in labor law. They were looking for an update and upgrade of their main logo and public image. The main goal was to design a classic styled logo yet modern and minimal and give it a new identifiable colors palette. It had to be, also, authentic, distinguishable, sympathetic and highly communicative. The company wanted it to transmit trust, firmness and loyalty to their clients.

The Logo

The Logo is a classic C+E combined, in a chic, unadorned and concise form. It brings easy recognition and still a strong personality. The variants change only by the colors, depending the usage each logo might be blue on white background, or keeping the background blue the logo highlights over it in white, Another option is the same logo with the Firm's full name under it on a chosen typography.

Colors Palette

The colors palette was inspired by the historical meaning in the French flag, standing for LIBERY, TRUST and RESPONSIBILITY. All these are key to the Firm’s in every case they work with... In a Legal Company the colors must be of easy recognition but also be characterized with seriousness, so I kept it to only 2 colors, having a variant in black for formal text in documents.

Typography

Main typography used in the logo and accents. It states Chosen typography for the name. It follows a similar style as the main font, but keeping a more classic latin shape. This was a bold classic serif typography. Selected for text and descriptions. This font represents a good readability and the trustworthiness of the brand.
